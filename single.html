Henry Cavill as The Witcher's Geralt is quite the transformation

Faisal Choudhury ⋅ 150 Reviews ⋅ 12 hours age

Henry Cavill is in The Witcher (Image: Netflix)

Superman himself is set to star as Geralt in Netflix's The Witcher. Henry Cavill is a sight to behold as the streaming site drops its first photo of the star. The teaser shows Cavill on set with silky, silver locks that even Legolas would be jealous of. Based on the best-selling fantasy series, The Witcher tells the tale of Geralt of Rivia, a solitary monster hunter, who struggles to find his place in a world where people often prove more wicked than beasts. "When destiny hurtles him toward a powerful sorceress, and a young princess with a dangerous secret, the three must learn to navigate the increasingly volatile Continent together," says Netflix.

Netflix also confirmed Twin Peaks' Eamon Farren as Cahir and Knightfall's Joey Batey as Jaskier. House of Cards' Lars Mikkelsen as Stregobor, Royce Pierreson as Istredd, Maciej Musiał as Sir Lazlo, Wilson Radjou-Pujalte as Dara, and Harry Potter's Anna Shaffer as Triss. The teaser doesn't show much more than Cavill so we still are in the dark as to what to expec.
